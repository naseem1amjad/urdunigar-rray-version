Usage of SpaceBar to distinguish between multiple words.

 

Summary: You should always press “Space Bar” key after you complete a [whole] word while typing in Urdu.

 

Example 1:

Following keys are pressed to write the sample statement in Urdu.

“io kia krrhy hw?”

 

To elaborate the usage of space bar in above text, have a look at following keystrokes.

“io[space]kia[space]krrhy[space]hw?”

 

 

 

By using the “spell check” option (by clicking on button) or going through the menu ( Tools>Spell Check)

 

You will get following

 

 

This is because the spell checking is being done on “KarRahay” and this word is not part of the dictionary/lexicon. The correct method is to enter “Kar Rahay” (note: the space here) {i.e. “Kar[space]Rahay”}

 

So for proper entry use following keys to write the sample statement in Urdu.

“io kia kr rhy hw?”

 

To elaborate the usage of space bar in above text , have a look at following keystrokes.

“io[space]kia[space]kr[space]rhy[space]hw?”
