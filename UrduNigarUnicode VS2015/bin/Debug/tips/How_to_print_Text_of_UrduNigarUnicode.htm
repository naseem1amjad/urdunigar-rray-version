Usage of MS Word Pad or MS Word for changing look & feel and printing text of Urdu Nigar Unicode

 

Summary: You can type text in “Urdu Nigar Unicode” and then copy from there and paste to Microsoft Word Pad or any other Unicode Complaint word/text editor.

 

 

In Menu Goto Edit >Select All , or right click on textbox and select “Select All” Option. Or press Ctrl+A (hotkey/shortcut key)

 

From Menu Select Edit>Copy option, you may use Ctrl+C as hotkey

Start MS WordPad and use “Paste” option [Ctrl+V hotkey]

 

Change the formatting of the text.
